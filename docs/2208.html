INTRODUCTION TO ETHICAL HACKING:
Introduction about ethical hacking and and basic aspects of the hacking was handled by Sumanth. Sumanth explained about what is hacking,what is meant by the ethics,and what is the difference between hacking and cracking. He also explained about how the saved passwords in the system can be exploited by the attacker and the passwords can be stolen,and what are the precautions people has to take to protect themselves from these type of attacks. After that, Udayadithya explained the classification of hackers,and about some of most commonly seen methods of hacking such as keyloggers,phishing,DOS and DDOS,brute force attack,etc. He also talked about the Kali Linux,and its use in the field of ethical hacking.

CRYPTOPARTY:
Udayadithya handled the session on cryptoparty. He explained cryptoparty is the set of techniques,which one could use to protect himself from the cybercrimes. He dug into the topic starting from global survivallence,its good as well as ill effects. Then he talked about 'steganography',a technique of hiding data behind images. The next topic, creating a strong password,and the criterias that are to be necessarily followed for that purpose was found to be useful. Then ,he moved on to the topic maintaining anonymity online. He enlightened people about the 'TOR' browser,and the 'deep web' and how they work . He talked about 'tails operating system',the OS which works in a flash drive and leaves no traces in the system. He also talked about 'ransomwares and viruses',the malicious softwares which are harmful to your computer and privacy. Then at last, he talked about the hacktivist forums,like 'anonymous and lulzsec' and their motto and ideologies,and with that he wound up the session.
