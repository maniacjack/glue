FSMK has been conducting summer camps for a long time now. Our summer camps
are known for fun,knowledgeable and Freedom oriented. But in summer camps, many people
does not get the opportunity to participate due to limited number of seats. To overcome this
problem and spread our philosophies to remote level, we have thought of conducting regional
camps.
As there was a overwhelming majority in the number of participants and
enthusiasts from Hassan, we have decided to conduct the regional camp in Hassan.This month,
from February 22, Thursday to February 28 ,Sunday ,a four day regional camp is going to be
held in Government Engineering College ,Hassan.Algorithms, database management,Operating Systems,javascript and many more exciting topics will be covered in this camp, by professionals
and highly experienced resource persons.
To increase the quality of the process, we are limiting the number of participants to a
maximum of 100, and out of them 40% will be reserved for hosting college. If you are
interested and excited about this, and like to attend the camp, registrations will be open until
2 'O' clock of February 20.
